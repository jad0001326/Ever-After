## Production checklist

- Use vertical 1080 × 1920 for Reels and Stories; keep important text out of roughly the top 14% and bottom 20% so interface controls and link stickers do not cover it.
- Use portrait 1080 × 1350 for feed carousels.
- Put the hook on screen in the first second of every short video.
- Add subtitles even when a voiceover is used.
- Keep Reels to one idea and one call to action.
- Use only venue imagery EverAft or the venue has permission to publish. Screen recordings and branded type cards are the safe fallback.
- Do not describe starter figures as averages, quotes or “what weddings cost”. Call them editable planning examples.
- Reply to useful comments with the relevant deep link rather than the homepage.
- Review Analytics after seven days using campaign `starter_budgets` and `regional_venues`.

## What to measure

Track reach and views for awareness, but judge the campaign mainly on:

- link clicks and engaged sessions;
- starter budget opens;
- shared starter budgets;
- venue profile views from regional pages;
- planner starts and venue imports;
- saves, shares and useful comments.
